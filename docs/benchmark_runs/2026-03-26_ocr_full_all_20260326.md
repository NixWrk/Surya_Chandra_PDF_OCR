## Quality Review Checklist

1. Open HTML report and compare engine snippets.
2. Open `texts/*.txt` and manually inspect key pages (title page, TOC, formula-heavy page).
3. Check typical OCR errors:
   - broken Cyrillic letters,
   - merged words/lines,
   - dropped headings and page numbers.
4. Choose final engine by weighted criteria:
   - quality first,
   - then runtime,
   - then memory.
